What is Achoo?

  • Achoo is a free health monitoring app designed to provide insight into your respiratory issues.

How Achoo Works

  • Data

    Collects data

    Achoo does this by retrieving information about the environment around you that may be contributing to your respiratory problems: air quality, allergens, and weather.

  • AI

    Applies artificial intelligence

    It then applies artificial intelligence models that have been tuned specifically with your health in mind. These models use the data around you and your treatment data to determine if environmental factors play a role in your outbreaks.

  • Breathe

    Helps you breathe better

    If the models detect that your outbreaks are initiated by environmental factors, it notifies you so you can get out in front of it.
